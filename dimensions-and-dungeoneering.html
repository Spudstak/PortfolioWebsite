General Overview

Dimensions & Dungeoneering is a minecraft modpack that has amassed over 12,500 downloads over its lifetime. Its primary focus is on adventure and combat, and it features things like custom classes, a custom questbook containing over 1000 quests that were made by me, and many various tweaks and changes to mods included in the pack.

My Role and Contributions

Was responsible for all aspects of development though I did occasionally ask for feedback from some friends in discord.
