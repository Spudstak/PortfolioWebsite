General Overview

This game is a multiplayer arena FPS where players must elimate other players by killing them a sufficient amount of times so that they can no longer respawn. The goal of the game is to be the last player standing. Each time a player dies they will drop a gear at their death point. This gear can only be obtained by the player that died and functions as a currency to buy powerups to make your character stronger throughout the game. These powerups persist between rounds and can be used to gain a competitive edge against your fellow opponents, be careful though as they can get stronger also. If you do not get back to your death point within a sufficient amount of time your gears will despawn.
