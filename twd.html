General Overview

The Walking Dead: Betrayal is a third person action game of cooperation and deception for up to 8 players. Out of resources, with a herd of walkers fast approaching, players must work together to complete objectives and make their escape. However, incognito traitors lurk among them, plotting to do everything in their power to delay repairs and prevent anyone from leaving.

My Role and Contributions

Things that I worked on were typically code related and included things like bug fixing, implementing various items, implementing various roles, the gates for the safezone, the fire damage effect, crafting recipies, displaying map information, etc.
